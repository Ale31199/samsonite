KEY BENEFITS

IMPRESSIVELY RESILIENT, UNIMAGINABLY STRONG

Shells are made from Roxkin™, an innovative material that bounces back into shape easily and is highly resilient resisting shocks. It is exceptionally robust, thanks to its phenomenal layer-by-layer in-built strength.

MADE IN EUROPE

Developed by our experienced research team and made at our own production plant, Roxkin™ material is a symbol of innovation and quality.

INTEGRATED USB PORT

Stay connected, anyplace, anytime. Your devices can easily be charged using the integrated USB port.

This suitcase is not sold with a power bank. Be sure you comply with the airlines rules regarding the usage of Power Banks.

OUTSTANDING COMFORT

Enjoy a carefree, premium travel experience with the elongated double tube pull handle and smooth rolling double wheels, ensuring ultimate ease of use and stable manoeuvring.

PRACTICAL INTERIOR WITH EXPANDABILITY

The two spacious compartments come with removable cross ribbons, to keep all your belongings neatly in place. The large pocket on the divider pad and hidden expandability offer extra practical storage.
